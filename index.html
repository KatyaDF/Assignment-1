PROJECT IDEAS

Overview

My project idea is an app where you can store your dog's microchip details It would also have a scanning technology where you can scan other dog's microchips, you could use this when you find a lost dog and then their details would come up on your phone and you can return the dog to their owner. This could also be used if you meet someone with a dog and they let you scan their dog's microchip to find out more information about them. I would have this linked to a social networking profile as well so your dog can add other dog's they meet on their walks and keep in contact. This app would also be used to search for your dog's siblings as litters have similar microchip numbers and then the dogs can reunite with their siblings.

Motivation

Dogs have become increasingly popular in the last decade, 62% of people in Australia have a pet, we have almost 4.2 million pet dogs in Australia. This app would be very useful as anyone who has lost a dog, which for most people is like a child know how stressful that situation is. If someone is able to so easily locate where the missing dog has come from it'll be easy for them to be reunited with their owners. I myself have always wondered where my dog's siblings are and wether they'd recognise each other if they were to reunite, I can imagine many other people would wonder the same thing. I would love for my dog to meet his brothers/sisters.

Description

Own personal dog profile - This would list your dog's microchip number, name, address, contact number, likes/dislikes, allergies, medical history etc. Microship scanner - You can scan dog's microchip to read their personal details, wether it is out of interest or you want to add them on your dog's socials or the dog is lost, I would keep in mind to have a feature where you can block your address out to certain people. Social media - Sort of like a dog Facebook where you can upload photos and statuses. Microchip search - You can search microchips from your dog's litters and organise catch ups. Map - I would include a map that shows you the nearest dog park/dog beach/nearest dog event/nearest dog frinedly establishment and it would show you which other dogs are there or how busy the park is (if their owner has chosen to tag their location.)
